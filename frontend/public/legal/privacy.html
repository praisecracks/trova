Statutory Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

Trova.ng commits fully to protecting the operational datasets of Nigeria social merchants and their checkout buyers. This privacy charter details the systems we enforce to keep user accounts secure and trade documents completely safe.

1. Information We Collect

In order to initialize secure multi-party escrow link instances, we collect the minimum necessary operational variables:

  • Merchant Full Name, profile label, active bank settlement data, and email indices.
  • Shopper Mobile Phone numbers and delivery coordinates.
  • Custom item nomenclature, weight specifications, and escrow pricing parameters.

2. Use of Captured Datasets

We do not lease or sell merchant data pipelines to high-volume commercial marketing aggregates. Information harvested serves uniquely to:

  • Verify identity details to eliminate duplicate fraudulent accounts.
  • Deliver real-time carrier delivery SMS updates securely to Nigerian carriers.
  • Clear payouts immediately following buyers' approval checks on physical specs.

3. Asset Security Protection

Hold funds are handled exclusively through segregated Central Bank of Nigeria approved trade vaults. Database records utilize bank-grade encryption protocols both at rest and in transit preventing arbitrary third-party tampering.
