Terms of Escrow Service

Effective Date: May 25, 2026

This statutory document regulates the creation, deposit holding, transit checking, and disbursement processing of payments conducted through the Trova.ng platform inside Nigeria.

1. Escrow Deposit Arming

By executing secure, payment-ready links on this platform, buyers agree to authorize swift deposit holdings kept directly inside segregated trustee banking reserves. The seller cannot touch these funds, and the buyer cannot withdraw them arbitrarily without formal delivery feedback.

2. Delivery Inspection Term

Following logistics arrival or courier delivery notices, buyers hold exactly 48 hours to inspect items for specification and quality compliance. If the timeline passes without explicit input, the settlement clears automatically to the seller's GTBank, Zenith, or Access account.

3. Arbitrations Protocols

In cases where either party raises an active shipping dispute on sizing or item differences, neutral Trova mediation specialists will review parcel dimensions, dispatch proofs, and communication logs. Decisions rendered by our mediators are final and binding.
